Simple Features¶

  • OpenGIS Simple Features Access (ISO 19125) — http://www.opengeospatial.org/standards/sfa

  • OpenGIS Simple Features for SQL — http://www.opengeospatial.org/standards/sfs

Definuje vektorové datové typy

  • Bod

  • LineString

  • Line

  • LinearRing

  • Polygon

  • GeometryCollection

  • MultiPoint, MultiLineString, MultiPolygon

A operace (metody) nad těmito geometriemy

Dále způsob zápisu ve formátech WKT (Well Known Text) a WKB (Well Known Binary).
